## Design Decisions

- Used custom card-style buttons instead of URadioGroup for richer visual feedback (per MUST NOT DO)
- Integrated `useCheckpoint()` composable for `solved` state sync
- Submit button disabled until selection made; "Try Again" button appears after submit for retry
- Letter badges (A, B, C, D) via `String.fromCharCode(65 + index)` with semantic color states
- Focus-visible ring for keyboard accessibility (`focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-primary`)
- `fieldset[disabled]` prevents option changes after submission
- Icons from `i-heroicons` collection (already in serverBundle config)
